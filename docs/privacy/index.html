Privacy Policy for SurgiVitals

Last updated: March 7, 2026

SurgiVitals is designed to work offline-first for veterinary anesthesia monitoring. This Privacy Policy explains what information SurgiVitals may store and how it is used.

Information We Collect

SurgiVitals stores case and monitoring data that you enter, such as patient details, vital records, timestamps, and notes.

How Data Is Used

Data is used to provide app features including case history, monitoring views, and PDF export.

Data Storage

Monitoring and case data is stored locally on your device. If you share or export a PDF, that shared file is handled by the destinations you choose.

Third-Party Services

SurgiVitals does not sell personal data. App distribution and crash diagnostics may be provided through Apple platform services.

Your Choices

You can delete cases from within the app. You can also remove the app from your device to remove local app data.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@raynevlabs.com
